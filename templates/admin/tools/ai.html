How pricing works and why it’s monthly

  • One-time setup: initial SEO structure (titles, meta, JSON-LD, sitemap). Done once.
  • Ongoing improvements: search rewards freshness. We rotate and expand content so you don’t slide down.
  • AI automation: per plan we auto-refresh metadata, FAQs, and JSON-LD on a schedule.
  • Review mode: choose auto-publish or “draft for approval” inside the admin.
  • What you’re paying monthly: scheduled AI updates, location/service expansion, logs, and crawl pings.
Result: SEO is not “set once.” It stays alive and competitive with automated updates.
